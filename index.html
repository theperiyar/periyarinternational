About Us

Our Mission

Periyar International propagates the humanistic ideas of Periyar E.V.Ramasamy to the world, promoting self-respect, social justice, and rational thinking. Read More

Our Organization

Founded in 1994 in Chicago, Periyar International now has chapters across the world, uniting humanists, rationalists, and social justice advocates. Country-specific chapters can be formed according to local rules with the consent of Dravidar Kazagam and our operational director. Read More
